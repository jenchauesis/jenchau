Selected Work

PROJECT 001
◆ FASHION DESIGN

MacArthur

This collection is my way of blending the strange beauty of insects with the epic, haunting vibe of science fiction. I was inspired by the alien elegance of bug anatomy, mixed with the vast, philosophical feel of The Mote in God's Eye. Every outfit is like a character—some are warriors, some are ghosts, some are gods. I wanted the clothes to feel like they're performing on their own, like they came from a distant world with their own myths and histories. It’s not just fashion—it’s a visual performance.

Wolfie

Discover the allure of my 'Wolfie' collection, inspired by my fascination with wolves. The design ethos stems from the moment I envisioned the wolf, radiating an inherent sophistication. Each piece encapsulates the wolf's noble spirit, translating it into a harmonious blend of refinement and wild charm. Immerse yourself in this collection that elegantly captures the untamed grace of the wolf.
